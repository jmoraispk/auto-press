## Ideas

### #1 — "Sometimes I just don't want to sit at my desk."

Tone: honest, slightly self-deprecating. Anti-productivity-bro.

**Shot:** on the couch / in bed. Phone in one hand, book or coffee
in the other. Definitely not at the desk.

**Beats (60 s):**
1. *(camera on you, lying down)* "I know I should be at my desk
   coding. I just… don't want to."
2. *(hold up phone showing the CodeAway PWA)* "So I'm not. The
   agent's working. I'm here."
3. *(quick cut to laptop in another room, dark screen, code
   compiling)* "It pings me when it's stuck. I read what it said.
   I type the next thing." *(send a reply from the phone)*
4. *(back on couch)* "And then I keep reading."

**Hook:** relatable laziness > "developer productivity hack".
Nobody wants a productivity tool; everybody wants to do less.

**Distribution:** TikTok / Reels / Shorts vertically. Repurpose
to landscape for Twitter / LinkedIn. **Do not** post on HN as a
video — for HN, the artefact is "Show HN: CodeAway" with the
repo link and the existing landing.

## Themes worth mining

- **Honesty over polish.** "I was on the couch and didn't want to
  get up" beats "10x your dev productivity" by a mile in 2026.
- **Agent loops as the gap.** Everyone with Cursor or Claude Code
  knows the rhythm: 3 min of agent work → 10 sec of clicking
  Continue → 3 more min → 10 sec → repeat. That's the wedge.
- **Phone as the lazy person's interface.** A laptop demands
  posture. A phone doesn't. That's the whole product in one
  sentence.
- **"I built this for myself."** Pre-empts the "is this even useful?"
  question — answer: yes, for me, and that was enough.

## Production parking lot

- **Length:** aim 60 s, edit ruthlessly. Most "60 s" ideas land at 90 s.
- **Aspect ratio:** vertical 9:16 first, crop horizontal for Twitter / dev.to embeds.
- **Audio:** no music to start. Honest-dev tone benefits from silence.
- **Editing tool:** CapCut on the phone is enough. Don't over-tool.
- **Title for the YouTube long version:** *"I built a Cursor remote for my phone so I could stay on the couch"*
- **Show HN title:** *"Show HN: CodeAway — drive your Cursor / Claude Code agent from your phone"*

## Open questions

  for HN comments and curious converts, but a much bigger lift.
  Maybe the existing YouTube demo is already that.
- First post timing — Monday morning US Eastern (HN's softest
  algorithm) vs. whenever you have energy?
- Naming — do we ever shift from "CodeAway" to something more
  searchable? Too late to matter probably.
